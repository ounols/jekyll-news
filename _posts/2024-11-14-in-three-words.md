Life has a way of continuing, regardless of what happens to us. In three simple words: **it goes on**. This isn't a statement of resignation, but rather an acknowledgment of life's resilience and our own capacity to adapt and move forward.

## The Continuity of Life

No matter what challenges we face—loss, disappointment, failure, or change—life continues. The sun rises each morning, seasons change, and the world keeps turning. This continuity can be both comforting and challenging.

## Finding Meaning in Continuity

Understanding that life goes on helps us:

- **Perspective**: Our problems, while real, are part of a larger flow
- **Resilience**: We can recover and rebuild after setbacks
- **Hope**: Tomorrow brings new possibilities
- **Acceptance**: Change is the only constant

## Living in the Flow

When we accept that life goes on, we can better navigate its ups and downs. We learn to ride the waves rather than fight against them, finding peace in the natural rhythm of existence.

## Conclusion

In three words: it goes on. This simple truth reminds us that we are part of something larger, something that continues beyond our individual experiences. And in that continuity, we find both comfort and strength.
